### Vocabulary


ἐπειδή when, since, after

ὅς, ἥ, ὅ  (relative pronoun) who, which, that

τίς, τί (interrogative pronoun) who? what? which?

τις, τι (enclitic indefinite pronoun) someone, anyone, something, anything, a certain person/thing
